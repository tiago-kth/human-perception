v

Welcome!

Thank you for taking part in this experiment! It is really quick, it will only take about 5 minutes. It is completely anonymous and we won't collect any data from you, other than the answers you provide to the questions below.

After the short form below, you will be presented to six different excertps of songs of about 30s duration, chosen from a Brazilian musical genre known as "Choro" or "Chorinho". You will then be invited to pick the color (or colors, you can pick more than one) that you think best fits that particular musical excerpt. At the end, you just click on "Send" and that's it!

You can listen to the audio clips as many time as you want. As mentioned, you can select more than one color. Every time you pick a color, the background will change, to help you visualize that color, or that color combination. If you want to discard/deselect one color that is already selected, you simply click on its swatch again.

Ah, if you like the songs, we can point you to a nice album on YouTube where you can listen more "Chorinho" (but only after you press "Send" — ha!)

First, some questions about yourself! :)

  • To which gender identity do you most identify?
  • Do you (or did you) play an instrument?
  • Do you have any experience with creating music?
